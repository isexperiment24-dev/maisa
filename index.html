Our Mission

Building the future of human-AI collaboration in software engineering

01

Project Overview

Developing comprehensive frameworks and standards for effective human-AI collaboration in software engineering. Our goal is to establish hybrid intelligent practices that optimize decision-making and enhance overall development efficiency.

02

Focus Areas

  • Human-AI collaboration protocols
  • AI governance frameworks
  • Training and skills development
  • Decision-making optimization
  • Business model innovation
03

Expected Impact

Targeting 30-40% resource savings in software development through transformative AI integration. Bridging theoretical research with practical application to redefine software engineering for the AI era.

Research Questions

Research Questions 1 & 2
What frameworks or standards are needed to facilitate effective human-AI collaboration in software engineering, and how can these frameworks be adapted to different environments and project needs?
Research Questions 3 & 4
What are the most effective strategies for using AI in software engineering decision-making, and how can AI be integrated to automate routine tasks while ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and ethical standards?
Research Questions 5 & 6
What training methods are most effective for equipping software engineers with skills for AI collaboration, and how can these methods be evaluated and improved for evolving AI-enhanced environments?
