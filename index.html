üî• Key Focus Areas

  • Human-AI collaboration protocols
  • AI governance frameworks

üìà Expected Impact

ü§î Research Questions

RQ1 & RQ2
What frameworks or standards are needed to facilitate effective human-AI collaboration in software engineering, and how can these frameworks be adapted to different environments and project needs?
RQ3 & RQ4
What are the most effective strategies for using AI in software engineering decision-making, and how can AI be integrated to automate routine tasks while ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and ethical standards?
RQ5 & RQ6
What training methods are most effective for equipping software engineers with skills for AI collaboration, and how can these methods be evaluated and improved for evolving AI-enhanced environments?
